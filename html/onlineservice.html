Online Zoom Fellowship

We are excited to host a vibrant and engaging weekly online fellowship through Zoom Meetings, designed to connect and uplift youths who may not have physical access to our services. Our goal is to create a dynamic virtual space where young people can come together, be inspired, and build meaningful connections.

Biblical Sermons

Experience thought-provoking sermons that provide spiritual insights and practical wisdom to guide and motivate you in your daily life.

Interactive Games

Dive into fun and engaging games that not only entertain but also foster teamwork and friendly competition.

Talent Hunt

Showcase your unique talents or cheer on your peers as they share their gifts and passions, all for the glory of God. It's a wonderful opportunity to celebrate and support each other’s creative expressions.
